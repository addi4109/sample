Department of Automobile Engineering

About Course

The Department of Automobile Engineering was established in the year 1983 with the objective of imparting quality technical education and practical skills in the field of automobile engineering. The department offers a three-year Diploma programme in Automobile Engineering.

The programme provides strong fundamentals in automobile systems, internal combustion engines, vehicle maintenance, and manufacturing processes with emphasis on hands-on training.

The department is committed to producing competent diploma engineers capable of meeting industry requirements, higher education, and entrepreneurial opportunities.
