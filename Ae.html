Department of Automobile Engineering

Automobile Engineering

Established: 1983

Vision: The vision of Automobile Engineering Department is to grow the students into engineers and entrepreneurs with elevated skills, who can accumulate the global demands and input concepts and knowledge of automobile engineering.

Mission: The mission is to input concepts and knowledge of automobile engineering and develop psycho-motor and soft skills by providing global-standard facilities and programs.

  • SATTEP Lab
  • Auto Engines Lab
