Department of Automobile Engineering

About Course

The Department of Automobile Engineering was established in the year 1983 with the objective of imparting quality technical education and practical skills in the field of automobile engineering.

The department offers a three-year Diploma programme in Automobile Engineering which provides strong fundamentals in automobile systems, internal combustion engines, vehicle maintenance, and manufacturing processes.

Emphasis is given on hands-on training, industry exposure, and development of professional skills to meet current technological demands.

Infrastructure

  • Well-equipped automobile laboratories
  • Departmental library with reference books
  • Modern workshop with tools and testing equipment
  • Seminar hall for technical activities and guest lectures

Course Offered

Diploma in Automobile Engineering

  • Duration: 3 Years
  • Intake Capacity: 60 Seats
